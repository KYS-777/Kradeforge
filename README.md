# Kradeforge

## Professional Trading Journal & Performance Analytics Platform

Kradeforge is a modern trading journal designed for traders who want to track, analyze, and improve their performance without manually entering every trade.

Import broker exports, visualize performance through interactive charts, monitor risk metrics, and discover insights from your trading history.

---

## Features

### Automatic Trade Import

Simply drag and drop your broker export file.

Supported formats:

* CSV
* XLSX
* XLS

Supported brokers:

* Interactive Brokers
* TD Ameritrade
* E*TRADE
* Robinhood
* Webull
* Tastytrade
* Schwab
* Fidelity
* TradeStation
* Generic CSV

Kradeforge automatically detects the broker format and imports trades into your journal.
